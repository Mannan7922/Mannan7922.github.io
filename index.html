About Fortnite

Epic Games is the developer of the well-known online video game Fortnite. Debuting in 2017 it comes in three different game modes: Fortnite: Save the World a cooperative hybrid tower defense-shooter and survival game that allows four players to defend objects with built-in traps and fortifications against zombie-like creatures Fortnite Battle Royale a free-to-play battle royale game where players compete to be the last person standing and Fortnite Creative where players have unrestricted creative control over creating their own worlds and battle arenas.

Ever since its debut Fortnite Battle Royale has amassed a massive player and viewer base generating billions of dollars in revenue from in-game purchases and microtransactions. It is renowned for its vibrant visuals quick gameplay and regular updates that bring in new material and keep the experience engaging for users.
