About Rank

The Fortnite Ranked system is based on a familiar set of medal insignias, beginning with Bronze and maxing out at Unreal. Your initial rank is determined after playing one match, and the difficulty level will depend on previous skill-based matchmaking history. After that, youâ€™ll work your way up the ladder by gaining points each game. Points are earned on a game-to-game basis by eliminating players and gaining high match placement. And if you get to a high enough ranking, each game will begin you at a point deficit. Fortnite uses a tier ranking system, with ranks ranging from Bronze to Unreal. Players progress through different ranks as they gain points. The top-tier ranks are Elite, Champion, and Unreal, with Unreal being the ultimate goal. Players' initial rank is shown after one match, based on their hidden matchmaking performance. Newer players typically start with Bronze or Silver.
